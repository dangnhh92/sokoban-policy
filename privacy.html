Privacy Policy — Sokoban 1000 Levels

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sokoban mobile application (“the App”) handles information. The App is a free puzzle game and may display advertisements via third-party providers (e.g., Google AdMob). The App also offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to remove ads, processed by Apple through the App Store.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect personal information from users. However, third-party ad networks integrated in the App may collect certain data automatically, such as:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID), IP address, app interactions, and coarse location for ad delivery and analytics.
  • Crash, performance, and diagnostic data to improve service reliability.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to personalize and measure ads. For details, please review:

  • Google Ads & Cookies
  • AdMob Privacy & Data
  • Google Ads Settings (opt-out controls)

You can also limit ad tracking using your device settings (e.g., “Reset/Limit Ad Tracking” on iOS/Android).

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional non-consumable in-app purchase (“Remove Ads”) that permanently disables banner and interstitial advertisements for the purchasing Apple ID. All payment processing, receipt validation, and purchase restoration are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or process any payment information on our own servers.

Purchase status is stored locally on your device and, when you use the “Restore Purchase” option, is re-verified with your Apple ID via the App Store. For more information about how Apple handles purchase data, please review Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically to children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through ads or third-party services, please contact us so we can help address the issue.

Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any data collected by third-party services is retained according to their policies.

Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • Use device privacy settings to reset or limit the Advertising ID.
  • Visit Google Ads Settings to manage ad personalization.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
